- **Module 3**: The 12-column grid - this is THE foundation of Bootstrap. Master this and everything else is easy!
- **Module 4**: Build real layouts like sidebars and multi-column pages.
- **Module 5**: Center things, reorder things, make layouts responsive. Pure magic! ✨
- **Module 6**: Flexbox power - the secret weapon for flexible designs.

### 🟠 **Level 3: Working with Components** (Modules 7-11)
- **Module 7**: Typography - make your text beautiful without trying too hard.
- **Module 8**: Forms - create forms that users actually want to fill out.
- **Module 9**: Buttons - they're everywhere! Master all the styles.
- **Module 10**: Navigation bars - great nav = happy users.
- **Module 11**: Cards - the building block of modern web design.

### 🔴 **Level 4: Fine-Tuning Your Designs** (Modules 12-17)
- **Module 12**: Spacing (margins & padding) - good spacing = professional looking sites.
- **Module 13**: Colors - bring your designs to life with Bootstrap's color system.
- **Module 14**: Show/hide things on different screen sizes - responsive superpowers!
- **Module 15**: Borders, shadows, and polish - the details that matter.
- **Module 16**: Positioning - place things exactly where you want them.
- **Module 17**: Icons - 1000+ beautiful icons to make your designs shine.

### 🎓 **Level 5: Advanced** (Modules 18-19)

- **Module 19**: Customize Bootstrap for your brand - make it truly yours.
